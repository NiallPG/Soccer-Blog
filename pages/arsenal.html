Arsenal is a club based out of London. The club has been historically successful winning 13 league titles, but has never had the most trophies. Despite this, Arsenal have one of the largest fan bases in the world due in part due to the commentators surrounding the club and the general antics that usually goes on with the club. Though Arsenal have struggled in recent seasons, the club finally seems to be picking things up with smart player acquisitions. The club typically sports red and white.
