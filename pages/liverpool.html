Liverpool FC is a historically and recently successful football club based out of the western english city of Liverpool. Liverpool is owned by the Fenway Sports Group, owners of the Red Sox, and have won the champions league as recently as 2019. Although Liverpool has fewer trophies as compared to Manchester United and Arsenal, they have always had success domestically and internationally. Coached by one of the best coaches in the world, Jurgen Klopp, Liverpool represent a disaffected part of England with an “its us against everybody mentality”.
