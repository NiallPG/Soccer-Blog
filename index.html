This site is meant as a quick overview of the most popular soccer teams in Europe. As an American having lived in Europe, I experienced some of the wonderful soccer culture there, and by making this I intended for someone with little to no idea of the section of the sport, to get to know it a little. Having had to explain the culture to some of my less informed friends has allowed me to have a better understanding of how to sum the culture up to someone on the outside.
